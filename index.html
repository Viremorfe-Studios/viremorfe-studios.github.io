Our Phylosophy

The ram represents fire, cunning, strength, rebellion and courage, representing that Viremorfe Studios is a team that does not fear the adversities that may stand in its way. We are independent, we are rebels, and we are passionate about taking risky decisions.

Our Enigmatic Games

Shadead

Shadead is a procedural psychological horror game inspired by the Penumbra saga. Solve puzzles that change every time you play, within a shifting atmosphere and narrative, creating a unique "dynamic horror" experience.

Expected launch date: In development

Engine: Godot Engine

Genres: Psychological Horror - Puzzles - Thriller

Game modes: Single player

Expected platforms: Steam - Itch

Our Strange Workflow

We truly believe that Open-Source software is the most sustainable model for long-term software development, and this includes game development. That's why we use technologies like Godot, Blender, LMMS, etc. Because they offer a high customization, comfort and versatility when it comes to something as complex as game development, and they allow us to focus on what is really important to us, the design of the game.
